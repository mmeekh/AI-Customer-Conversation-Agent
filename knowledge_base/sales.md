# Sales FAQ

## What plans do you offer?
We offer three plans: Starter ($29/mo, up to 5 users), Growth ($99/mo, up to 25 users with priority support), and Enterprise (custom pricing, unlimited users with SLA and dedicated CSM).

## Do you offer a free trial?
Yes, all paid plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You can cancel anytime during the trial.

## Can I see a demo before committing?
Absolutely. Book a 30-minute demo at clemta.com/demo. We tailor the walkthrough to your use case (sales ops, finance automation, or custom workflows).

## Do you have annual discounts?
Yes. Annual billing saves 20% compared to monthly. Enterprise customers can negotiate multi-year discounts.

## How does your pricing compare to competitors?
Our Growth plan is roughly 40% cheaper than Zapier's equivalent tier and includes built-in AI agents at no extra charge.
