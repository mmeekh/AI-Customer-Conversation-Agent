# Billing FAQ

## How do I update my payment method?
Log in -> Settings -> Billing -> Payment Methods. Click "Add new card" or "Update existing". Changes apply to the next billing cycle automatically.

## What if my payment fails?
We retry the charge three times over 7 days. After the third failure, the account is paused but data is retained for 30 days. Update your card to resume service immediately.

## How do refunds work?
Refunds are issued for unused service time on annual plans within the first 30 days. Monthly plans are non-refundable but can be cancelled anytime to stop future charges.

## Can I get an invoice for a past period?
Yes. Settings -> Billing -> History. PDF invoices are available for the last 24 months.

## How do I change my plan?
Settings -> Billing -> Plans. Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period.
